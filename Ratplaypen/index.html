About Rat Playpen

Rat Playpen strives to educate people about our furry little friends giving the proceeds of our sales to Shelters for Rats, because of the revulsion many people have toward wild rats, pet rats are often mistreated,Seen as vermin and misunderstood. pet rats commonly get no respect at many place and therefore don't get the care they need or deserve.
